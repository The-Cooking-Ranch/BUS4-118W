Vegan Tacos

Prep: 10 mins | Cook: 15 mins
Serves 6
Here’s a delicious, flavourful, and summery vegan taco recipe for ya! Made with a simple vegan ingredients. It’s simple yet colourful and so flavourful. The perfect dinner for an evening fiesta.

Ingredients

  • 12 hard or soft taco shells
  • 1 1/2 cups cooked ground beef or vegan alternative, prepared with taco seasoning
  • 8 ounce vevan ched-shred
  • Optional toppings: shredded lettuce, avocado slices, chopped tomato, diced onion, pico de gallo, guacamole, cilantro, salsa, sour cream or non-dairy alternative
  • Cilantro (optional garnish)
  • Lime wedges (optional garnish)

Instructions

  • Heat taco shells according to directions.
  • Fill warm shells with meat or vegan alternative, Vevan Ched-Shred and additional toppings, as desired.
  • Garnish with cilantro and lime wedges, if desired.
