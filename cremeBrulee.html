Creme Brulee

Prep: 10 mins | Cook: 30 mins
Serves 5
This incredible Crème Brûlée looks fancy, but is quick and easy to make! This dessert has creamy custard filling and a caramelized sugar topping. Top Crème Brûlée with fresh berries for a delicious show-stopping dessert.

Ingredients

  • 6 egg yolks
  • 6 tbsp white sugar, divided
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 1/2 cups heavy cream
  • 2 tbsp brown sugar

Instructions

  • Preheat the oven to 300 degrees F (150 degrees C).
  • Beat egg yolks, 4 tablespoons white sugar and vanilla extract in a mixing bowl until thick and creamy.
  • Pour cream into a saucepan and stir over low heat until it almost comes to boil. Remove the cream from heat immediately. Stir cream into the egg yolk mixture; beat until combined.
  • Bake in a preheated oven for 30 minutes. Remove from the oven and cool to room temperature. Refrigerate for at least 1 hour or overnight.
  • Preheat the oven to broil.
  • In a small bowl combine remaining 2 tablespoons white sugar and brown sugar. Sift this mixture evenly over custard. Place the dish under the broiler until sugar melts, about 2 minutes. Watch carefully so as not to burn.
  • Remove from heat and allow to cool. Refrigerate until custard is set again.
