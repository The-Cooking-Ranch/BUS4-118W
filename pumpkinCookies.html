Vegan Pumpkin spice cookies

Prep: 25 mins | Cook: 7 mins
Serves 4

  • 2 1/2 cups all purpose flour
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 tsp pumpkin spice
  • 2 tsp cornstarch
  • 3/4 cup vegan margarine
  • 1/4 cup pumpkin puree
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 tbsp non-dairy milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 1 1/2 cups mini chocolate chips
